Tralee Driving School


Tralee Driving school is a approved learner driver school based in Tralee Co.Kerry, Tralee Driving Schools aim is to teach learner drivers the basics of driving to allow them to easily pass there driving test and obtain their full driving license. Tralee driving school was founded is 2005 and has one of Irelands highest first time pass rates.
